About Me

Passionate advocate for inclusive education and technology accessibility

Hello, I'm Arjuna Caleb Gyan

Founder & Director of EduAccess

My Journey

I’m a student with a strong interest in learning, technology, and education. I’m passionate about finding creative ways to make learning more inclusive and accessible for everyone. I enjoy exploring new ideas, solving problems, and using technology to help people overcome challenges. Outside of academics, I love reading, discovering new skills, and sharing knowledge with others. My goal is to keep learning, growing, and making a positive impact wherever I can.

Through EduAccess, I aim to bridge the gap between traditional education systems and the unique needs of students with disabilities. By combining cutting-edge technology with evidence-based inclusive teaching practices, we're creating learning environments where every child can thrive and reach their full potential.

Key Achievements

  • Donated to the Cape Coast School for the Deaf and Blind.
  • Presented an innovative assistive tool for children with disabilities to La Plage Factory.
  • I am currently in discussions with the Global Rainbow Foundation to co-develop an innovative solution for children with disabilities.

Skills & Expertise

Technical Skills

Educational Technology

Expert in assistive technologies including screen readers (JAWS, NVDA), speech recognition software (Dragon NaturallySpeaking), alternative input devices, and adaptive learning management systems (Canvas, Moodle)

Curriculum Design

Specialized in Universal Design for Learning (UDL), creating WCAG 2.1 AA compliant content, developing multi-sensory learning materials, and designing accessible digital resources

Data Analysis & Research

Proficient in measuring learning outcomes, conducting educational research, analyzing program effectiveness through quantitative and qualitative methods, and using data visualization tools

Web Development & Accessibility

Skilled in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, ARIA standards, accessibility testing tools (WAVE, axe), and building WCAG-compliant educational platforms and digital learning tools

AI & Adaptive Learning

Experience with AI-powered educational tools, machine learning for personalized learning paths, natural language processing for communication aids, and adaptive assessment systems

Gamification & Engagement

Expertise in creating engaging, accessible educational games, implementing reward systems, designing interactive simulations, and using VR/AR for immersive learning experiences

Soft Skills

Empathy & Understanding

Deep commitment to understanding individual student needs, creating safe and supportive learning environments, and advocating for students' rights and dignity

Communication & Collaboration

Excellent at building relationships with educators, families, students, and stakeholders; skilled in presenting complex ideas clearly and facilitating productive team discussions

Problem Solving & Innovation

Creative approach to overcoming educational barriers, designing novel solutions to complex challenges, and adapting strategies based on student feedback and outcomes

Leadership & Mentorship

Proven ability to inspire and guide teams, mentor educators and students, lead organizational change initiatives, and build consensus around inclusive education practices

Continuous Learning

Committed to staying current with latest research, emerging technologies, policy changes, and best practices in special education and accessibility

Cultural Competence

Understanding of diverse cultural perspectives on disability, experience working in multicultural environments, and commitment to equity and social justice
